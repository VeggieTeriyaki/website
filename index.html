About Me

From day one, my passion has been in engineering products that impact a large number of people on a personal basis. This is why I started out as a mechanical engineering major. However, as time went on, I realized a computer could help me do the exact same thing, just much faster. Since then, I have fallen in love with technology and coding and there has been no looking back. Whether it be developing software to make my college peers' life better, or to help out a social cause in Europe, I love building highly scale-able and powerful systems. If this sounds like something your company does, feel free to reach out to me for a summer 2019 internship!
